Privacy Policy for Temporary Notepad

Last updated: January 27, 2026

Data Collection

Temporary Notepad does not collect, transmit, or share any user data. All notes are stored locally in your browser using Chrome's storage API.

Data Storage

  • Notes are stored locally on your device only
  • Dark mode preferences are stored locally on your device only
  • No data is sent to external servers
  • No personal information is collected

Data Retention

Notes are automatically deleted when you restart your Chrome browser. Dark mode preferences persist across browser sessions but can be cleared by uninstalling the extension.

Third Parties

We do not share data with third parties because we do not collect any data.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date.
